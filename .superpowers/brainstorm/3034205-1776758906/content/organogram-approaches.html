A — Card Grid

Workgroups as structured cards, people listed inside with role colors. Static, clean, readable. Easy to export as image. Works great for 10+ workgroups.

B — Network Graph

Workgroups as nodes, people as nodes, edges connecting them. Dashed lines show people in multiple groups. Dynamic — nodes can be dragged. Beautiful but harder to read at scale.

C — Radial / Hub-and-Spoke

Community at center, workgroups radiating out, people on the spokes. Visually striking, shows community structure as a whole. Gets crowded with many members per group.
