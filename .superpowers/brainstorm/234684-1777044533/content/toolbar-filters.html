Select all the controls you want in the toolbar:

1

By person / By workgroup toggle

Switch between single-node and clone-node modes. Already decided — just confirming it lives here.

2

Filter by workgroup

Dropdown to isolate one workgroup — dims or hides everyone else. Useful for "show me only the Finance cluster."

3

Filter by role

Highlight only people holding a specific role (e.g. "Voorzitter"). Others dim out.

4

Hide unavailable toggle

Remove sick/vacation people from the graph entirely. Useful for "who can I actually reach right now."

5

Show / hide aspirants

Aspirants shown with dashed outline by default. Toggle to hide them for a "full members only" view.

6

Show / hide names

Names below nodes clutter the graph with 45+ people. Toggle to show only dots, hover to reveal name. Cleaner for overview.

7

Search / spotlight person

Type a name → that node pulses and centres, everything else dims. Great for "where is Lilian in this graph."

8

Export as SVG / PNG

Already exists in the current views. Keep it here too for sharing/printing the organogram.
