CORE (Community Organisation and Roles Engine) is a W3DS platform for managing
community membership, roles, workgroups, and availability. It's built around
an interactive organisation chart — a live picture of who's in your community,
which workgroups exist, and who holds which role.

## As a community member, you can

### See who's in the community
Open the organogram — the main view when you log in — and switch between a
graph and a card layout. Filter by workgroup, by role, or by membership type,
search by name, and toggle whether to show inactive members. Click any person
or workgroup to open a detail panel with their bio, contact info, roles, and
availability. You can also export the card view as an image.

### Manage your own profile
Edit your display name, bio, email, phone number, website, and location, set
your birth date, and upload an avatar and a banner image. You can copy your
own W3DS eName from your profile at any time.

### Join and manage workgroups
Browse workgroups and join or leave them yourself. Within a workgroup you've
joined, you can assign or unassign yourself to its named roles. If a
workgroup has a linked chat, leaving it will ask whether you also want to
leave that chat.

### Set your availability
Mark yourself as available or unavailable, with an optional reason and an
"until" date, or clear your status when it no longer applies. Other members
can see your current status from the organogram.

### Manage a workgroup you help run
If you've been made an admin of a specific workgroup, you get access to that
workgroup's management tools — editing its name, description, and color, and
managing its roles and members — without needing full community-admin
access.

## As a community admin, you can

- **Manage community details** — edit the community's name, slug,
  description, KvK (chamber of commerce) number, and legal form; upload a
  logo, group photo, or statutes file; set a brand color and title font;
  promote or demote other community admins.
- **Manage members** — add someone to the community by looking up their
  W3DS eName, edit a member's membership type and join date, remove a
  member, or copy any member's eName.
- **Manage all workgroups** — create or delete workgroups, edit their
  details and colors, create/edit/delete roles, and add, remove, or
  reassign members and roles across any workgroup in the community.
- **Manage availability for anyone** — see everyone who's currently marked
  unavailable, set or clear availability on a member's behalf, and define
  the availability types (name and emoji) members can choose from.
- **Link the community to a W3DS eVault** — connect your community to its
  own eVault so its identity and membership data sync to it, or unlink to
  stop that sync.

## Why your data isn't locked into CORE

Your profile, membership, workgroup, and availability data live in your own
eVault, not only in CORE's database — CORE is one interchangeable frontend
onto data you own. If a community links to its eVault, that same data is
visible to other W3DS-aware platforms without you having to re-enter it
anywhere else — for example, a workgroup's chat can be picked up by a
separate chat platform without CORE providing chat itself.
